Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 can of carrots
  • 1/2 can whole new potatoes, drained

Directions

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Cook and stir onion, flour, salt, and pepper in melted butter until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes..
Remove skillet from heat and pour chicken broth and milk into the skillet;
bring the mixture to a boil and cook to thicken slightly, about 1 minute. Remove skillet from heat and stir chicken,
peas and carrots, and potatoes into the broth mixture.
